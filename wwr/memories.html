Memories

Lucy

I was a little scared at first, I think you can see it on my face, it was my first time rafting. However, then the fear went away and I took with me a great memory. I would be more than happy if my family would take me rafting again.

Christina

My sisters and I went on vacation this summer and decided to venture into the world of rafting. An unforgettable memory.

Mark

I think I've lost count of the number of times I've been rafting. I just love it. I think I will grow old and never stop rafting, or at least as long as my body will let me. My rafting team and I ventured out on one of the most challenging courses Water World has to offer.
