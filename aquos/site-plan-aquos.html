Overview

Purpose

Our purpose is to advertise and sell showers with an exceptional development, a new system that will help you, your friends and your family to save water. You can also find information on how they work and their price range. This website will also provide a way for you to contact us and find our stores or the official distributors of such products.

Audience

Our website and products will be targeted to people between the ages of 25 and 65 who are homeowners. It will also be targeted to people who care about the environment and want to save water. These people may be interested in smart homes. Establishments where many showers are used, such as gyms, sports facilities, etc., will also be able to purchase our products. The website can be accessed from any device.

Store

Your shower as you had never imagined.

Imagine you jump out of bed and the shower is waiting for you at the perfect temperature.

Imagine that, with a simple gesture, you can regulate it, change the flow rate or stop it to soap up, and that after that time you can continue to enjoy the water in the same conditions.

Advanced design.

Intuitive use. Its capacitive technology and the design of its controls make the Smart Shower very easy to use.
